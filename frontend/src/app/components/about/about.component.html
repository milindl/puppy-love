Who said win-win situations do not exist?
Puppy Love helps you meet your crush, anonymously.

Express your interest, and both of you will be informed if the interest is mutual. Otherwise, no one will EVER know about your choice. No, not even the admin, i.e. us.

Features

If you do not match with someone, no one can ever know your choice, not even the administrator No information will ever leave your browser which lets the administrator or anyone else see your choices Your login password (of puppy love) will never be sent to the server, even for login. We use secure hashes. Algorithm reviewed by professors and peers in terms of security and confidentiality
